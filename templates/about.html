Mix'n Bowls
What started as a small idea to meet the baking needs of a local town has, 12 years later expanded to include three successful stores and hundreds of happy customers! Mix'n Bowls have had a high street prescence in Kendal, Penrith and Ulverston where we have offered a friendly and personable service that keeps our customers coming back. We now want to bring this same experience to online shopping for baking needs. Our website is due to launch late summer 2021 and whilst this is underway we decided to offer the baking community a place where they can share their very best and favourite baking recipes. This is Community Treats! So please do stay, have a look around and sign up so you can enjoy the full functionality of the site.

In the meantime please see below, the products we will be bringing to our online store front in the very near future.
